How a week grows here

Three unhurried stages, on repeat — no other order makes sense for a garden.

i

Sow

Drop your tasks and goals into the week as seeds — just a title and a bed to rest in.

ii

Tend

iii

Bloom

Come Sunday, tended seeds open into blooms — a soft, visual record of the week you kept.
